Dear Sir/Madam,

**Re: Application for Capital Markets Services License — Venture Capital Fund Manager (VCFM)**

We write to submit an application for a Capital Markets Services License under the Venture Capital Fund Manager (VCFM) regime on behalf of Pefko Capital Management Pte. Ltd. (the "Company").

The Company has been incorporated in Singapore as a private company limited by shares for the purpose of managing the Pefko venture capital fund ("Pefko" or the "Fund"), a Cayman Islands Exempted Limited Partnership that will invest in early-stage deep-technology ventures.

We confirm that:

1. The Company will solely manage funds that qualify under the VCFM regime — investing at least 80% of committed capital in securities directly issued by unlisted business ventures incorporated for no more than 10 years at the time of initial investment.

2. The Fund will only be offered to accredited investors and/or institutional investors and will not be continuously offered for subscription or redeemable at the investor's discretion.

3. The Company has appointed a minimum of two (2) Responsible Officers who are ordinarily resident in Singapore and meet the fit and proper criteria prescribed by MAS.

4. The Company maintains a permanent physical office in Singapore.

5. The Company has established compliance arrangements, including an AML/CFT framework in accordance with MAS Notice SFA 04-N02.

Please find enclosed the completed Form 1V together with all supporting documents as detailed in the checklist appended hereto.

## 6. BUSINESS PROFILE AND ACTIVITIES

### 6.1 Investment Strategy

The Company will manage the Pefko Fund, a deep-technology focused venture capital fund targeting early-stage and growth-stage technology ventures across Asia and globally. The fund's strategy focuses on:

- **Sector Focus:** Deep technology — AI/ML infrastructure, advanced computing, semiconductor design, quantum technologies, robotics, advanced materials, synthetic biology, and cleantech
- **Stage Focus:** Seed to Series B, with follow-on capability through Series C
- **Geography:** Primarily Asia-Pacific, with selective opportunities globally
- **Check Size:** US$1M–$5M initial, with reserves for follow-on
- **Portfolio Construction:** 15–25 investments over a 4-year investment period

### 6.2 Qualifying Investment Compliance

All fund investments will be qualifying investments under the VCFM regime:
- 100% of investments will be in securities directly issued by unlisted ventures
- All portfolio companies will be incorporated for no more than 10 years at the time of initial investment
- The Fund will not invest in listed securities or IPOs
- At least 80% of committed capital (excluding fees and expenses) will be deployed in qualifying investments

### 6.3 Investor Base

The Fund will only be offered to:
- Accredited investors as defined under Section 4A of the SFA
- Institutional investors as defined under Section 4A of the SFA

The Fund will not be:
- Offered to retail investors
- Continuously available for subscription
- Redeemable at the investor's discretion

- **Compliance Officer:** [NAME] has been appointed as the Company's compliance officer responsible for monitoring and ensuring ongoing compliance.
- **External Compliance Support:** [NAME OF COMPLIANCE FIRM, if applicable]

### 8.3 Investor Disclosure

In accordance with the VCFM regime requirements, the Company will provide full disclosure to all investors and potential investors that:

(a) The Company is licensed as a Venture Capital Fund Manager and operates under the simplified VCFM regime;
(b) The Company is not bound by all the regulatory requirements imposed on other categories of fund management companies (e.g., Licensed Fund Management Companies under the full regime);
(c) Specifically, the Company is not subject to minimum base capital requirements, risk-based capital requirements, or certain ongoing business conduct rules that apply to other fund managers; and
(d) Investors should consider these factors when making investment decisions.

### 8.4 Annual Declarations

The Company will submit the Annual Declaration (Form 25A) within one month of the end of each financial year, as required by MAS for VCFMs. The Company notes that VCFMs are not required to submit financial statements or auditors' reports to MAS.
