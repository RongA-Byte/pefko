# RESPONSIBLE OFFICER PROFILES TEMPLATE

## Pefko Capital Management Pte. Ltd.
## For MAS VCFM License Application

---

**Purpose:** This template is to be completed for each proposed Responsible Officer ("RO") of Pefko Capital Management Pte. Ltd. in connection with the application for a Venture Capital Fund Management (VCFM) license from the Monetary Authority of Singapore ("MAS").

**Requirement:** Under the VCFM regime, a minimum of two (2) Responsible Officers must be appointed. Each RO must be ordinarily resident in Singapore.

**Note:** Under the VCFM regime, directors and professionals are not required to have a minimum of 5 years of relevant fund management experience, unlike the requirements for Licensed Fund Management Companies (LFMC) under the full regime. However, relevant experience is a positive factor in MAS's assessment.

## MAS ASSESSMENT CRITERIA FOR RESPONSIBLE OFFICERS

For reference, MAS assesses Responsible Officers against the following criteria under the Guidelines on Fit and Proper Criteria [FSG-G01]:

### Honesty, Integrity, and Reputation
- No criminal convictions for dishonesty offences
- No history of fraud, misrepresentation, or misconduct
- Good reputation and character

### Competence and Capability
- Relevant educational qualifications
- Professional experience appropriate for the role
- Understanding of the regulatory framework
- Track record in fund management or related field
- (Note: Under VCFM, the minimum 5-year experience requirement for LFMCs does not apply)

### Financial Soundness
- Not bankrupt or subject to arrangements with creditors
- No outstanding judgments or awards
- Financial standing adequate for the role

### Other Considerations
- Compliance record with regulatory authorities
- No disqualification from directorships
- No current investigations or pending proceedings

## NOTES FOR COMPLETION

1. **All fields marked [BRACKETS] must be completed** with actual information before submission to MAS.

2. **Supporting documents** should be certified true copies where indicated. Certification may be by a Singapore-qualified lawyer, Justice of the Peace, or notary public.

3. **Non-English documents** must be accompanied by certified English translations.

4. **Foreign qualifications** may need to be verified through the issuing institution or a recognised credential evaluation service.

5. **MAS may request additional information** or clarification on any aspect of the RO profiles during the application review process.

6. **Changes to ROs** after license issuance must be notified to MAS within 14 business days.

7. **Concurrent roles** are permitted under the VCFM regime — for example, the CEO may also serve as Executive Director, Responsible Officer, and MAS Representative.
